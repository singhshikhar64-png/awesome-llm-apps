FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a product quote? +
Fill in the contact form above (select "Request Quote" tab), specifying the product, estimated quantity, and delivery location. Our sales team responds within 2 business hours with pricing, MOQ details, and lead times. For urgent quotes, call +91-20-4000-1234.
Do you offer international shipping and export documentation? +
Yes. MedVance India handles end-to-end export logistics including EXIM documentation, customs compliance, CE/FDA certificates of conformity, cold-chain shipping for temperature-sensitive devices, and DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) terms for select markets.
Can I become an authorised distributor? +
We actively seek distribution partners in Africa, MENA, South-East Asia, and LATAM. Select "Distributorship" in the contact form and tell us your region, existing medical device portfolio, and client base. Our partnership team will arrange a video call within 48 hours.
What regulatory approvals do your products carry? +
All products carry CDSCO (India) approval. Most carry CE Mark (EU MDR), and flagship products have FDA 510(k) clearance. WHO Prequalification is available for select diagnostic products. Country-specific regulatory dossiers can be provided on request for import registration.
Is on-site installation and training included? +
For capital equipment (patient monitors, imaging systems), installation, commissioning, and one-time in-service training for clinical staff are included in the purchase price. Annual maintenance contracts and preventive maintenance visits are available separately.
What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ) for consumables? +
MOQ varies by product. Consumables such as surgical kits, implant sets, and disposable accessories typically have an MOQ of 50‚Äì200 units per SKU. Instruments and capital equipment have MOQ of 1 unit. Sample/evaluation units are available for qualified buyers.
